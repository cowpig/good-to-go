Rin Kaiho (Ishigure Ikuro)
Ishigure Ikuro

In his first round match against Rin Kaiho, Ishigure Ikuro was in byo-yomi, and placed his stone just when his time had run out. He was judged to have lost, but lodged a protest and a replay of this game was ordered. Rin Kaiho did not show up for the replay, considering that he had won already. Thus, Ishigure Ikuro won by forfeit. Unhappy to have won in such a way, he did not show up for his game in the second round, and lost by forfeit.
