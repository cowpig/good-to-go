Ricoh Pro Pairgo Tournament

This tournament was held 15 times, from 1994/1995 until 2008/2009, with the match in December of one year and the final early the next year. Here we label the tournaments with the year of the final.

This page gives the winning pairs. The linked pages give the full tournament.
