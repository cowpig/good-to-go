Women's Kakusei Finals

The Women's Kakusei (or Female Kakusei) tournament has been held 24 times, first in 1979, last in 2002 (with final in 2003). It was a knockout tournament. Komi was 5.5. According to SL "it was played with short time limits (10 minutes, 30 seconds byoyomi) in the earlier rounds, but with longer ones in the final and semi-finals". In the first year, the final was a best-of-three match, later just a single game. Here we give the finals.
