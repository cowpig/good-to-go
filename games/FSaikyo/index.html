Women's Saikyo Finals

The Women's Saikyo tournament (Female Saikyo, Women's Strongest Player, Tokyo Seimitsu Cup) has been held 10 times, first in 1999, last in 2008.

Komi was 5.5 in the first four editions, and 6.5 in the last six. In the last edition each player had 1 hour time, plus 60 seconds/move. Maybe this was 1 hour plus 30 seconds/move earlier.

The tournaments were knockout tournaments, starting with 32 players (but 16 in the last year). In the last year, the final was a best-of-three match, earlier just a single game. Here we give the finals.
